We are a Community Devoted to Fighting World Hunger by Reaching out to Local Vicinities. One Step at a Time, we are starting from the FCT, Abuja.

Our Mission

Our mission at Feed The Street With MURPHY is to combat hunger by providing nutritious meals and essential support to vulnerable communities. We aim to inspire hope and create lasting change by addressing food insecurity and fostering a spirit of compassion and care.

Healthy Food

We provide and distribute nutritious food and essential food items to vulnerable children, homeless individuals, and underprivileged communities on the streets, promoting health and well-being for those in need.

Kids Education

We empower and support the educational journey of gifted streets children by providing access to quality schooling, mentorship, and resources, enabling them to realize their academic aspirations and reach their full potential.

Pure Water

We strive to ensure access to clean and safe drinking water for vulnerable street children, reducing the risk for waterborne illnesses and promoting their overall health and well-being through our water provision initiatives.

Medical Care

We deliver comprehensive medical outreach services, including healthcare screenings, treatments, and preventive care, to vulnerable individuals, particularly sick children and expectant mothers in the streets.
